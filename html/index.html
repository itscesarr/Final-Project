College Playlists

Hello World. My name is Cesar, I am a senior at the University of Michigan studying at Stamps and UMSI. This website is a tribute to all the music and playlists that I have listened too throughout my time in college. Now that I am at my last stretch, I wonder if my music would help others, just as it helped me. Hope you like it!

Music is Therapy

I believe that music has a power that provide sustinance in every occassion––good or bad. My goal is to provide as much music as possible in hopes that I could deliver that sense of refuge for students looking for a method to cope from an endless stress from school, work and commitments.
